**Can adpositions have person/number marking?**

Adpositions supplement the case system by providing extra ways of expressing temporal, spatial, and other types of relationship, e.g., in the Hungarian example (1a), *mellett* conveys a spatial relationship. Adpositions also share several characteristics with cases, for instance, they may be used as stems to which personal suffixes are attached. This question examines weather person/number marking is possible on words that are also used as adpositions (see 1b).
